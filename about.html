12+ Years of Excellence in Hair Artistry

I am Azeez Oyindamola Elizabeth, a creative and passionate hairstylist with over 12 years of hands-on experience in the beauty industry. My journey began in 2013 as a freelance hairstylist, and today I proudly serve as the CEO and Lead Hairstylist of OfficialRetouch Beauty Studio in Ikorodu, Lagos.

My expertise combines technical skill with artistic creativity to enhance client confidence and beauty. I specialize in wig making, braiding, frontal installations, dreadlocks, and natural hair care, always staying current with the latest trends and techniques in the industry.

What sets me apart is my commitment to not just styling hair, but transforming lives. Every client who sits in my chair receives personalized attention and a service tailored to their unique beauty and lifestyle needs. I believe that great hair is the foundation of confidence.
